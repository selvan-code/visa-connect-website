United States Visa Services

Expert guidance for tourist, visitor, business, and work visas for applicants traveling to the United States.

VisaConnect Assis offers professional assistance for U.S. visa applications, helping individuals, families, and professionals prepare accurate documentation and confidently complete the visa process.

US Visa Categories

US Tourist Visa (B2)

For individuals traveling to the United States for tourism, holidays, sightseeing, or leisure activities. We assist with documentation preparation and interview guidance.

US Visitor Visa (B2)

For applicants visiting family members or friends in the United States. We assist with invitation letters, relationship proof, sponsor documents, and interview preparation.

US Business Visa (B1)

Designed for professionals attending meetings, conferences, training programs, or exhibitions in the US. We assist with employer letters and business invitations.

US B1/B2 Visa

A combined visa category allowing travel for both business and tourism purposes. We assist with DS-160 form filling, appointment scheduling, and interview preparation.

US H1B Work Visa

For skilled professionals employed by US-based companies. We assist with eligibility checks, employer documentation, and petition guidance.
